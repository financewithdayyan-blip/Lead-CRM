The Process

Three steps. No surprises.

We've simplified the process to remove as much friction as possible. Most sellers spend more time on the phone with their cable company than they do dealing with us.

1

Tell us about your property

Fill out the form on our site, call, or text us. Share the address, your situation, and any timing concerns. This takes five minutes and there's no commitment.

2

Receive a cash offer

We review the property using comparable sales, public records, and our own local knowledge. Within one business day you'll have a written cash offer — no strings attached.

3

Pick your closing date

If the offer works for you, we handle the paperwork and you choose when to close. We can move in as little as 14 days or give you more time — whatever fits your situation.

What We Stand For

How we do business.

We work with people during some of the most stressful moments of their lives. That shapes how we operate every day.

No hidden costs

There are no agent commissions, closing costs, or inspection fees on your side. The number we quote is the number that lands in your account.

We move at your pace

We don't push a close date that doesn't work for you. Need more time to sort out personal affairs? We wait. Need it done in two weeks? We move.

Real people, real conversations

You won't get a chatbot or an automated system. When you reach out, you talk to a member of our acquisitions team who knows your file.

Zero obligation to accept

An offer from us is just that — an offer. You're never locked in until you sign. If our number doesn't work for you, there are no hard feelings.
