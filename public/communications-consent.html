Communications Consent

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This page explains how Bluebird Acquisition contacts you by phone and text message, and how you can opt out at any time. It applies whenever you submit your phone number to us, whether through our website form, by calling our office, or by texting us directly.

1. Consent to Be Contacted

By submitting your phone number through our website, or by calling or texting us, you consent to receive calls and text messages from Bluebird Acquisition and its representatives at that number regarding your property inquiry, including offers, follow-up questions, and transaction updates. These communications may be sent using an automatic telephone dialing system or prerecorded voice.

2. Consent Is Not a Condition of Purchase

You are not required to consent to receive autodialed or marketing calls or texts as a condition of receiving an offer or doing business with us. If you prefer not to receive such communications, you can still reach us by phone or email using the contact details on this site.

3. Message Frequency and Charges

Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and any ongoing transaction. Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. Bluebird Acquisition does not charge you for text messages.

4. How to Opt Out

You can stop text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send you. You will receive a one-time confirmation that you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent unless you re-initiate contact. To stop phone calls, tell our representative directly during a call, or contact us using the information below and ask to be placed on our internal do-not-call list. We will honor opt-out requests within a reasonable time, consistent with applicable law.

5. Help

Reply HELP to any text message for assistance, or contact us directly at (217) 408-2781 or contactus@bluebirdacquisition.com.

6. Carrier Disclaimer

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Supported carriers are not guaranteed to include every wireless provider.

7. Calling Hours

We make reasonable efforts to contact you only during permissible hours under applicable federal and state law.

8. Relationship to Our Privacy Policy

This Communications Consent supplements, and should be read together with, our Privacy Policy.
