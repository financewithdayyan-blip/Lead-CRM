Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Bluebird Acquisition ("Bluebird," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, when you visit our website, call or text us, or submit a property inquiry.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, email address, property address, and details about your situation when you fill out our contact form, call our office, or correspond with us by text or email. We also automatically collect limited technical information when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages viewed, through standard web server logs and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: evaluate and respond to your property inquiry; prepare and communicate purchase offers; verify property and ownership details through public county records; contact you by phone, text, mail, or email about your inquiry; and improve our website and services. We do not use your information to make automated decisions that produce legal effects without human review.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share information with: title companies, escrow agents, and closing attorneys involved in a transaction you initiate with us; service providers who help us operate our business (such as CRM, phone, and SMS platforms), bound by confidentiality obligations; affiliated entities or assignees in connection with a purchase agreement; and government agencies or other parties when required by law, subpoena, or court order.

4. Text Messages and Phone Calls

If you provide your phone number, you may receive calls or text messages from Bluebird Acquisition regarding your property inquiry. See our Communications Consent page for full details on consent, message frequency, and opt-out instructions. Message and data rates may apply.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including to maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If we make you an offer that does not result in a closed transaction, we may retain your information to follow up on future opportunities unless you ask us to delete it.

6. Your Choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, opt out of marketing calls and texts at any time (see Communications Consent), and unsubscribe from email communications using the link in any email we send. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. State Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under state privacy laws, including the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information as those terms are defined under applicable law. See our Do Not Sell My Info page to submit such a request.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website or services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
